Platform capabilities

Everything your
team needs, unified.

From the first patrol check-in to the final incident report — SentryOne handles every step of your operational workflow.

Patrol Management
Schedule, track, and verify guard patrols in real time. GPS checkpoints, missed-scan alerts, and full audit trails built in.
Incident Logging
Capture incidents with photos, notes, and timestamps. Structured workflows ensure nothing slips through the cracks.
Smart Reporting
Generate professional reports automatically. Scheduled summaries, custom exports, and client-ready formats in seconds.
Team & Permissions
Role-based access for guards, supervisors, managers, and clients. Each person sees exactly what they need to see.
Notifications & Alerts
Instant alerts for missed patrols, open incidents, and critical events. Configurable per site, role, and severity level.
Audit & Compliance
Immutable audit logs for every user action, login, change, and export. Stay confident during reviews, disputes, and compliance checks.

1
Set up your tenant
